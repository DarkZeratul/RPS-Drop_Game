To play the game, run it on Firefox and use your mouse or keyboard.

Mouse: 
- Move your mouse along the canvas to move yourself on the game
- Left click to change between rock, paper and scissor.

Keyboard
- Press the left or right arrow key to move yourself on the game
- Press the space bar to change between rock, paper and scissor.

You start with 45 seconds to score as many points as you can.
- Winning the rock, paper, and scissor gives you more 4 seconds.
- Losing the rock, paper, and scissor withdraws from you 4 seconds.
- Drawing the rock, paper, and scissor withdraws from you 1 seconds.
- Missing a rock, paper, and scissor withdraws from you 2 seconds.
